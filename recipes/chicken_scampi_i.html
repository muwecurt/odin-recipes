Chicken Scampi I

Return to homepage

Description

A wonderful, fresh, light way to serve chicken. Good over rice.

The ingredients are ideal for 5 servings.

Ingredients

  • 5 (4 ounce) skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves - cut into 1 inch strips
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup white wine

Steps

  1. Combine chicken with ground pepper, cheese, parsley, garlic, salt, oregano, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce and wine in a shallow bowl. Marinate in the refrigerator for several hours (overnight is best).
  2. Preheat broiler. Remove chicken from marinade (save marinade) and place in a shallow pan. Broil 8 inches from heat, turning once, until chicken is no longer pink inside.
  3. In a small saucepan, bring marinade to a boil. Pour over chicken, toss and serve. Yummy!
